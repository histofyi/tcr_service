Reading this

One row per structure. Each bubble is one CDR loop against one region of the groove: its area is the area of the patch, its colour the shape complementarity (Sc) of that patch — how well the two surfaces mesh. Reading down a column shows what one loop does across every peptide this receptor has been solved against.

An empty cell means that loop never reaches that region. A white bubble means it does, but Sc could not be computed for the pair.

Hover a row for the numbers; click it to select that structure.
